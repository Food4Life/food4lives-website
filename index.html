Welcome To

Food For Life Initiative

In Georgia, our poverty statistics can be overwhelming. According to U.S. census data, Georgia is second worst state in the nation for child homelessness, and we are sixth in the nation for states with the highest number of unsheltered people. Together, we can feed hungry people and change this grim statistic. We can reach families in need with food and other essentials that offer hope for a better future. We don't live in a perfect world, and there's never going to be a perfect time to give—but there are always people out there in need of help.
Join us to win the fight against homelessness and the underfed .

Our Volunteers

We are Thankful to Large Community of Altruist Volunteers

I like doing selfless service (seva) because it shows me the power of giving. I get to give several loving hours out of my week to bring joy to hundreds of people. It’s also a friendly reminder that we’re all human beings and that we’re all on this planet for only a short while.

Leo Volunteer

Food4Life is a faith-driven initiative that seeks to provide Atlanta homeless with both physical and spiritual nourishment through freshly prepared meals. I joined the Food4life team so that I could serve others outside of myself. Among the hustle and bustle of my own daily life, it is easy to forget the plight of others. But every Saturday, I have the honor of preparing hot meals with a sincerely caring group and then serving these meals face-to-face to the community. I hope that, through Lord Krishna’s grace and our helping hands, we are able to feed some souls with good food and God’s love.

Cyrstal Rathore Volunteer

Thanks for Food for Life team and ISKCON Community for doing such a great service and given me opportunity to serve. Every Saturday feeding vegetarian food for hundreds of homeless is a great service, by volunteering here gives me positivity, motivates and inspire me to do more service in all the possible ways.

Nagesh Volunteer
