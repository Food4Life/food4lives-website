Adopt A Day

Thousands of Georgians won't have a meal on their table this Thanksgiving. At Food4lives we are committed to extending this generosity throughout the year. Please give generously and sponsor a day of the year in memory of someone, for your birthday or any special occasion to fill plates with food and hearts with hope everyday of the year.


Every dollar you give helps provide 100 meals.
